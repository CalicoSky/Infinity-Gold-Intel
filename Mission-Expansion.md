1. Expanded Mission Statement  
Infinity Gold Intel LLC formally declares one of its core missions to be:  
“Identifying, educating, and elevating young adults (18–25) and troubled teens (13–17) out of poverty and into full readiness for the next golden age through intensive education, life-skills training, mentorship, and real-world opportunity creation.”  

This mission operates under the banner  
Golden Age Readiness Program (GARP)  
and will be executed either as a program of the LLC or as a future 501(c)(3) “Infinity Gold Foundation” (to be formed).  

2. Program Summary  
- Immediate stabilization (food, shelter, mental-health first response)  
- Accelerated education (GED/vocational + golden-age curriculum)  
- Practical intelligence and life-skills training adapted from agency modules  
- Paid internships and direct placement into Infinity Gold Intel pipeline or partner employers  
- Long-term tracking to ensure permanent escape from poverty  

3. Current Status  
As of November 23, 2025, the Golden Age Readiness Program is in active Research & Development and pilot-planning phase alongside the main agency. All activities are fully funded by Shane E Baldwin / Infinity Gold Intel LLC until separate nonprofit status or grants are secured.  

4. Legal & Operational Posture  
- All youth-related activities comply with federal and state laws (COPPA, mandatory reporting, background checks, parental/guardian consents).  
- Program graduates aged 18+ may be offered junior roles or scholarships within Infinity Gold Intel academy tracks.  
- Intellectual property, curriculum, and branding remain property of Shane E Baldwin and Infinity Gold Intel LLC.  

5. Authorization  
Shane E Baldwin, as 100% owner of Infinity Gold Intel LLC, hereby authorizes immediate internal development, fundraising, outreach, and pilot preparation for the Golden Age Readiness Program effective this date.
